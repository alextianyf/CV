About Me

Hi! I’m Alex (Yunfei) Tian, an international student from China with a strong passion for technology and innovation. I hold an undergraduate degree in Electrical Engineering and have been honored on the Dean’s Honour Roll, receiving multiple International Student Scholarships in recognition of my academic excellence. Currently, I am pursuing a Master of Applied Science at the University of Victoria, where I focus on Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, particularly Machine Learning and Deep Learning. Beyond my core focus, I have a keen interest in Robotics, Embedded Systems, and the Internet of Things (IoT), and I am excited about exploring their intersection with AI to solve real-world challenges.
